Registration

People can join the LIFE ENHANCEMENT TEAM as a Business Partner or Regular Partner. When a person buys the first Product Pack at 45,000 naira, he/she can register to become a member of the LIFE ENHANCEMENT TEAM, which qualifies such a person as our Cooperative Member & Business Partner with REAL LIFE STORES. Business partners can carry out B2B and B2C transactions.

Relationship Center

There shall be a designated location or point where stakeholders can purchase products in bulk to resell to consumers based on stipulated guidelines. Partners deserve assistance to manage their inventory by providing a readily available source of products. The center can also serve as a point of contact for new and old partners, providing them with access to training or support materials.

Referral Earnings

A sponsor partner is entitled to five thousand naira from the first product pack purchased by a downline partner

Product Pack Bonus

A product pack costs forty-five thousand naira. Four thousand five hundred naira is deducted from 14 successive purchases to generate a reward of sixty-three thousand naira on each pack a bid caller buys.

Retail Profit/Sales Reward Earnings

Incentives and other motivational tools are awarded to boost stakeholders’ performance and maintain a high level of quota attainment for active partners of the Life Enhancement Team. This could be in the form of tangible rewards or intangible rewards. Auctioneers can make a 25% profit and above from retail sales.
